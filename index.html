About Me

Davide Usberti, known in the digital world as 333revenge or UsboKirishima, is a promising Italian developer and hacker. Born and raised in Parma, Davide has been passionately immersed in the world of technology and cybersecurity from a young age. Currently, he is pursuing his studies in computer science at the Istituto Tecnico Industriale Leonardo da Vinci in Parma.

Davide's journey into the realm of competitive hacking and cybersecurity began with his involvement in various Capture The Flag (CTF) teams. He has been an active member of local teams such as Havce, affiliated with the University of Parma, and CodeVinci, the official team of his school. His dedication and skill were prominently showcased when he led CodeVinci to victory in the CodeVinciCTF-2024.

In addition to this notable achievement, Davide has participated in several other significant competitions. These include the Havce Beginner CTF 2023, m0lecon 2023, bi0sCTF 2024, and snakeCTF 2023. His performances in these events have not only honed his skills but also established him as a formidable competitor in the cybersecurity community.

Davide's expertise and potential have been recognized on a national level as well. He has successfully qualified for national contests such as olicyber.it and cyberchallenge.it, organized by the Italian National Cybersecurity Agency. These qualifications underline his advanced understanding of cybersecurity principles and his ability to tackle complex challenges in the field.

With a solid educational foundation, hands-on experience in competitive environments, and a passion for cybersecurity, Davide Usberti is poised to make significant contributions to the field. His journey reflects a blend of academic excellence and practical prowess, marking him as a young talent to watch in the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity.
